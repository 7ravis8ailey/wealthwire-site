## The Annual Fee Math

Why do we focus so hard on no-fee cards? Because a $95 annual fee requires you to earn at least $95 extra in rewards vs. a free card before you break even.

On a $95-fee card earning 2% vs. a free card earning 1.5%: you need to spend **$19,000/year** before the fee card wins. Most people don't get there.

The no-fee cards above are competitive for most normal spending levels.
