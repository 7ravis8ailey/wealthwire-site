## What SoFi Gets Right

### 1. Best-in-Class Savings Rate
SoFi's savings account earns **4.00%+ APY** with direct deposit — one of the highest rates available at any bank. The national average savings rate is 0.47%. At $10,000 in savings, that's roughly $400/year vs. $47/year at the average bank.

Without direct deposit, the rate drops to 1.20% — still above average, but not the headline number.

### 2. Real Welcome Bonus
New members who open a SoFi Checking and Savings account and set up direct deposit earn:
- **$50** with $1,000–$4,999/month in direct deposits
- **$300** with $5,000+/month in direct deposits

The bonus is deposited within 30 days of meeting the requirement. No hoops, no complicated terms.

### 3. No Fees — For Real
No monthly fee. No minimum balance fee. No overdraft fee. No ATM fee at Allpoint network. No incoming wire fee.

SoFi has genuinely eliminated the fee structures that cost traditional bank customers $300–$500/year.

### 4. Early Direct Deposit
SoFi releases your paycheck up to **2 days early.** If you're paid biweekly, you get 26 paydays — the same amount, just 2 days earlier each time. Useful for covering bills before your official payday.

### 5. FDIC Insurance Up to $2 Million
SoFi spreads your deposits across multiple partner banks, giving you FDIC coverage up to $2 million — 8x the standard $250,000 limit. Relevant for anyone with significant savings.

### 6. Ecosystem Integration
SoFi is a full financial platform: personal loans, student loan refinancing, investing, credit monitoring, and insurance — all from the same app. If you want to consolidate your financial life in one place, SoFi does this well.

## Where SoFi Falls Short

### Savings Rate Drops Without Direct Deposit
The 4% APY requires direct deposit. If you set up direct deposit and later remove it, your rate drops to 1.20% without warning. Important to know.

### Overdraft Coverage Is Limited ($50)
SoFi's free overdraft coverage is only $50 with SoFi Protect. Compare to Ally's $250 or Chime's $200. If you regularly run close to $0, SoFi's coverage is less robust.

### No Physical Branches
SoFi is 100% online. If you need cash services, in-person support, or notarized documents, you'll need to go elsewhere. Customer service is phone and chat only.

### Cash Deposits Are Complicated
Depositing cash isn't straightforward — you'd need to use a third-party service or deposit at an Allpoint+ ATM. If you regularly deal in cash, Chime handles this better.
