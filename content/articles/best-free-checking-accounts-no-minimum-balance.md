## Why Minimum Balance Requirements Are a Trap

Traditional banks use minimum balance requirements as a revenue mechanism. If your balance dips below $1,500, you pay the monthly fee. That fee is often $12–$15 — which means if you're already low on funds, you get punished for it.

Every account above charges $0 regardless of your balance. $1 or $10,000 — same fee: zero.

## How to Switch Banks (It's Easier Than You Think)

1. **Open the new account** — takes 5 minutes online
2. **Set up direct deposit at the new bank** — give your employer the new routing/account number
3. **Wait one pay cycle** to confirm direct deposit is working
4. **Move any autopays** (subscriptions, bills) to the new account
5. **Keep the old account open for 60 days** in case any stray transactions come in
6. **Close the old account** once everything is confirmed

Total time: about 30 minutes spread over 2 weeks.
