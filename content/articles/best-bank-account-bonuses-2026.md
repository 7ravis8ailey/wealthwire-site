## How to Maximize Bank Bonuses

**1. Stack them.** You can open accounts at multiple banks. Open SoFi for the $300, then Chase for the $300 — that's $600 total.

**2. Use the account long enough to receive the bonus, then decide if you want to keep it.** Most bonuses require 60–90 days of activity. After that, you're free to close or keep it.

**3. Watch out for early closure fees.** Some banks charge $25 if you close within 180 days of opening. Read the fine print before you pull the trigger.

**4. Check IRS rules.** Bank bonuses are taxable income. You'll receive a 1099-INT if you earn over $10 in bonuses in a year.
