## Description

You know your team is wasting time on repetitive tasks. You just don't know exactly which ones, how much time, or what to do about it.

That's what this audit fixes.

I'll map your entire workflow, identify every automation opportunity, estimate the time and cost savings for each, and hand you a prioritized roadmap you can act on immediately.

**What you get:**
- 60-minute deep-dive into your current processes (async or live call)
- Full workflow map showing where time is being wasted
- Ranked list of automation opportunities with estimated ROI for each
- Specific tool recommendations (n8n, Make, Zapier, or custom)
- A clear "start here first" implementation roadmap
- PDF report you can share with your team

**Who this is for:**
- Business owners spending 5+ hours/week on repetitive tasks
- Teams using 5+ tools that don't talk to each other
- Founders who want to automate but don't know where to start
- Anyone who's ever said "there has to be a better way"

**What happens after:**
Most clients use the roadmap to implement automations themselves, or hire me to build them. Either way, you walk away with a clear plan and real numbers.
